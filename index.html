About Me

I'm a self-taught photographer with a passion for travel and design.

I was born in Germany and moved to the United States as a kid. To keep me distracted, my parents gave me a camera. Since then, I look at the world through a lens. I've visited over 10 countries and taken almost ten thousand photographs, and I feel like I'm just getting started. From landscapes to personal portraits and exciting scenes, I love being able to capture a moment. Join me on my journey!"
